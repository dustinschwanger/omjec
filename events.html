Events & Workshops

Connect, learn, and grow with OhioMeansJobs Erie County

Types of Events We Host

Free events and workshops for job seekers and employers

Job Fairs

Meet multiple employers in one place. Bring your resume and dress professionally to make connections.

Career Workshops

Learn resume writing, interview skills, job search strategies, and professional development.

Computer Skills Training

Build essential computer skills for the modern workplace through hands-on training sessions.

Employer Hiring Events

Company-specific recruitment events where employers interview candidates on-site.

Training Information Sessions

Learn about available training programs, certifications, and educational opportunities.

Networking Events

Connect with other professionals, employers, and community organizations.

Preparing for Events

Make the most of your job fair or workshop experience

Bring Resumes

Print multiple copies of your updated resume for job fairs

Dress Professionally

Dress as you would for an interview to make a great impression

Bring Identification

Have your ID and Social Security card for on-site interviews

Be Prepared

Research employers and prepare questions to ask recruiters

Arrive Early

Get there early for the best opportunities and shorter lines

Be Professional

Smile, make eye contact, and use a firm handshake
